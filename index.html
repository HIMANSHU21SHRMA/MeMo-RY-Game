Lifelines Overview

  • 50/50 Lifeline: After making 3 correct matches, you unlock the 50/50 lifeline. When you use it, two cards will be highlighted, but only one is correct. You need to choose wisely between them.
  • Show Button: After completing 4 correct matches, the Show button becomes available. Clicking it will reveal the location of all cards for 700 milliseconds. Use this time to memorize their positions!
  • Hint Lifeline: After 6 correct matches, the Hint lifeline is unlocked. It will show you two cards that are a correct match, but only when no cards are currently selected.

Use these lifelines strategically to increase your chances of winning the game!
